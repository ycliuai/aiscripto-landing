1. Executive Summary & Market Pain Points

In today's fast-paced software development lifecycle, quality assurance (QA) is a significant bottleneck. Even with dedicated QA/TAE (Test Automation Engineer) teams, mid-to-large enterprises face three core pain points:

  1. "Massive manual testing is time-consuming and error-prone" - Thousands of test cases are slow and prone to human error.
  2. "Automation scripts are written too slowly" - ~10 hours per moderately complex test case.
  3. "Scripts break daily with UI changes" - Teams get stuck in an endless cycle of fixing rather than testing.

💡 AiScripto Solution: An AI QA Agent That 'Reads Human Language' and 'Self-Heals'

AiScripto is an enterprise-grade AI automated testing platform that combines Large Language Models (LLM) and micro-frontend control technology to solve these problems at their root. It has three core 'superpowers':

  • Turns 'Human Language' Directly into Scripts: QA engineers write test steps in plain language → AiScripto generates high-quality Playwright code in minutes (down from ~10 hours, -80% reduction).
  • An Unbeatable 'Self-Healing' Mechanism: When UI elements change, AiScripto uses visual and logical reasoning to auto-repair. 92% self-healing success rate eliminates script maintenance.
  • 🔥 [Enterprise-Exclusive Brain] Deep Knowledge Base Integration: Connects to internal documentation, SOPs, and issue logs → becomes an AI agent that truly understands your business → produces 100% accurate tests.
